FAQ

  • Will I lose my progress if I delete the app?
    Yes. All study data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app erases all progress. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.
  • How do I restore my Pro purchase?
    Go to Settings (gear icon) and tap "Restore Purchase." Apple will verify your original transaction and re-unlock Pro features at no charge.
  • What certs are covered?
    CompTIA A+ (Core 1 & Core 2), Network+, and Security+. All terms are balanced to official exam domain weights.
