Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2026

Acronym - CompTIA Cert Prep ("the App") is developed and maintained by Nathaniel Craiu ("we", "us", or "our"). This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All study progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using the app's built-in storage. We do not have access to this data.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Browsing history
  • Any form of personally identifiable information (PII)

Local Storage

The App stores your study progress (flashcard scores, streaks, exam dates, and settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the App, this data is permanently removed.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. This transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no ads in the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, it is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
